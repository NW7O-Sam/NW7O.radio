About Me

My name is Sam Reynolds, amateur radio callsign NW7O. I was originally licensed in 2016 as KM4WDK and then got a vanity, N7PSR, in early 2020. I tested for both my General and Extra in mid 2020 online with GLAARG VEC, who does some amazing work.

I am interested in most parts of amateur radio, but most of my interests at this moment focus on HF. I enjoy contesting and DXing on both SSB and FT8. Much of my HF operating is done remotely thanks to the Remote Ham Radio Youth program. This program allows me to remotely operate some of the best stations in the US completely free. Thank you to W2RE Ray for this oppurtunity!
